Generating Audio for Muted Piano-Playing Video using Computer Vision

The goal of the project is to generate musical transcripts and corresponding audio from muted piano playing videos. When playing the piano, visual representations such as the position of the hands, the position of the keys and the depth with which they are pressed correspond to the sound produced by the piano.

The project is divided into four stages:

Keyboard Extraction: use SIFT algoritm to extract the keyboard area from each frame.

Key Labeling: extract each black and white key from the keyboard and label them.

Key-press Recognition: determine whether the keys are pressed or not and and record the status of each key.

Generate soundtrack: generate audio according to the record of the status of each key.
